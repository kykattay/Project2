MISO GRILL was founded by Kylie Taylor, Deon Tutt, and Abbey Parchman. Based in Nashville, they wanted to bring something different to the city. All having a love for traditional Japanese sushi and foods. MISO GRILL is a place to enjoy your special occasions like Birthdays, Anniversaries, Achievements, etc. MISO GRILL is a family environment where everyone is welcome to seat down and enjoy some tasty sushi!
